CHOOICE LIMITED – One-Stop Eco-Friendly & Energy-Saving Solutions

CHOOICE LIMITED is a technology company on a mission of “Green Innovation, Enhanced Ecosystems.” We specialize in energy-saving planning & engineering, environmental protection equipment, smart water & gas management, and sludge minimization, delivering integrated solutions that drive sustainable development.

Certifications & Experience

Awarded the Guangdong–Hong Kong Clean Production Partnership Plan mark (Technical Services) for five consecutive years, CHOOICE boasts deep technical reserves, extensive construction experience, and a complete qualifications framework, serving over 1,000 clients.

Main Services

  • Energy & Environmental Consulting: Backed by a robust technical support team and veteran experts with thousands of enterprise engagements, we uncover the most valuable, tailored solutions for your business.
  • Eco-Equipment & Engineering: Solutions include phase-controlled energy-saving devices, magnetic-levitation blowers, industrial gas-saving systems (compressed air, blower, vacuum), wastewater treatment station design & upgrades, central cooling for heat-generating equipment, sludge drying units, and low-temperature evaporation for waste liquids.
  • Project Operation & Management: Professional operation of wastewater treatment stations, energy-saving equipment, and comprehensive Energy Performance Contracting (EPC) projects.

Future Outlook

Looking ahead, through continuous innovation and supply-chain enhancement, we strive to improve water environments, develop new water sources, ensure drinking water safety, and enrich urban ecosystems. We look forward to partnering with you—combining our quality products and expert services to make an even greater impact on environmental improvement.
